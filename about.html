Every Thread Has a Second Life

At Zero Waste Gujarat, we believe that every thread has a second life. We are a pioneering textile recycling startup based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, dedicated to transforming textile waste into valuable products and creating a sustainable future for generations to come.

Our Mission & Story

"At Zero Waste Gujarat, we believe that every thread has a second life"

Designing a cleaner, greener tomorrow, one recycled thread at a time

Founded with a vision to revolutionize textile waste management in Gujarat, Zero Waste Gujarat operates from a 2,000 sq ft facility in Ahmedabad's industrial area. Our mission extends beyond textile recycling - we are building a comprehensive waste-to-value ecosystem.

While textile recycling is our current focus, our future vision encompasses expanding to include biomass (waste cooking oil) to bio fuel conversion, plastic repurposing, and various other waste-to-value initiatives. We believe in creating a circular economy where nothing goes to waste.

Our Promise

Four pillars that define our commitment to sustainability and innovation

Zero Waste Commitment

We are committed to ensuring that no textile waste reaches landfills. Every piece of fabric that enters our facility is transformed into valuable products, contributing to a truly circular economy.

Local Impact

Based in Gujarat's industrial heartland, we create local employment, support regional businesses, and contribute to the state's sustainable development goals while building strong community partnerships.

Innovative Products

Through cutting-edge recycling processes, we create high-quality recycled fiber, nonwovens, sustainable packaging solutions, and generate verified carbon credits for environmental impact.

Climate Action

Every ton of textile waste we process prevents harmful emissions and generates carbon credits. We're actively contributing to climate change mitigation through measurable environmental impact.

Our Future Vision

While textile recycling is our foundation, we envision a comprehensive waste-to-value ecosystem that transforms multiple waste streams into valuable resources.
